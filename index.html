EventCa.st is an event planning toolOrganizers can can create an EVENT comprising many events spanning hours or days.
Anyone can SUBSCRIBE just by entering the EVENT #HASHTAG code.

SIMPLE, ANONYMOUS

No sign-ons or registrations. No emails. No friction. Create an AGENDA immediately.



SHARE, SYNC

Distribute your event easily. Anyone can SUBSCRIBE if they know your event's unique #HASHTAG. Changes made to an event sync to all subscribers. Great for pop-up events!

CUSTOMIZE

Once you have subscribed, you can customize the event to include only those events you want to attend.


STAY NOTIFIED

EventCa.st's notification widget will keep you on track. Your smarphone will tell you how much time you have left until the next event you want to attend. It will give you directions too.
